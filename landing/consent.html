SMS Consent & Messaging Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

What is PawPing?

PawPing is an SMS-based assistant for veterinary clinics. When a client calls a clinic and the call is missed, PawPing sends a follow-up text message offering to help with appointment booking, medication refills, and general inquiries.

How Consent is Obtained

Customers opt in to receive SMS messages by initiating a phone call to a PawPing-enabled veterinary clinic. When that call is missed (no answer or busy), the caller receives a single automated text message offering assistance. Any further conversation is initiated entirely by the customer choosing to reply.

  • Opt-in method: Verbal/implied — the customer initiates contact by calling the clinic.
  • First message: A single SMS is sent in direct response to their missed call.
  • Continued conversation: Only occurs if the customer replies to the initial message.

Message Frequency

Customers receive one automated SMS per missed call. Additional messages are only sent in response to customer-initiated replies. Message frequency varies based on the conversation.

Opting Out

Customers can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Once opted out, no further messages will be sent. Customers can opt back in by replying START.

Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on the customer's mobile carrier and plan.
